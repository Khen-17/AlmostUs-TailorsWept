Welcome

Stories written with emotions rather than facts are welcome on this page. This song examines love at its most vulnerable—when it's passionate, ephemeral, and unforgettable. It explores how love may seem timeless in the now and terrible in reflection using striking images and deep allusions. For everyone who has ever loved deeply, lost softly, and remained unchanged as the seasons changed, every lyric is a confession and every chorus is a reenactment of a memory.
